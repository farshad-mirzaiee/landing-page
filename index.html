Odin, the Allfather

Ruler of Asgard and seeker of knowledge, Odin sacrificed much in his pursuit of wisdom. From the roots of Yggdrasil to the halls of Valhalla, his influence shapes the realms.

The Powers of Odin

Odin sacrificed his eye at Mimir's well to gain eternal wisdom.

His ravens, Hunginn and Muninn, travel the world and bring him knowledge.

Gungnir, his spear, is perfectly balanced and never misses its mark.

Yggdrasil, the world tree, connects all realms under Odin's watch.

Wisdom is not given freely. It is learned through sacrifice, patience, and the courage to seek truth beyond comfort.

- Odin, the Allfather
